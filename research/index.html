研究 Research

Robotics, intelligence, and real-world systems

让智能真正进入物理世界

RobAI-Club 关注机器人系统、具身智能与可靠自主学习。我们把感知、规划、控制、学习和工程实现放在同一个闭环中，用真实问题驱动技术成长。

我们关注 What we explore

  • 机器人感知、定位、规划与控制。
  • 具身智能与机器人学习。
  • 面向真实环境的可靠自主系统。
  • 从算法原型到可运行的机器人系统。

Directions

研究方向 Research Directions

以下方向是当前站点的初始信息架构，具体项目和研究成果会随着资料整理逐步补充。

Mobile & Field Robots

Designing robots that perceive, plan, and act in changing real-world environments.

Manipulation

Learning robust grasping, manipulation, and interaction skills for useful robots.

Embodied Intelligence

Connecting sensing, control, learning, and physical embodiment.

Perception & Learning

Building data-driven models for visual perception, representation learning, and decision making.

Robot Learning

Exploring imitation, reinforcement, and multimodal learning for robot autonomy.

Reliable Autonomy

Making intelligent systems more generalizable, interpretable, and dependable.

How we work

从问题到系统 From Question to System

理解问题 Understand

从真实场景出发，明确任务、约束、评价指标和可复现的实验设置。

构建方法 Build

结合硬件、软件、数据和模型，把想法快速变成可以运行、测量和迭代的原型。

验证系统 Validate

在仿真和真实环境中持续测试，让系统更稳健、更可解释，也更接近实际应用。
