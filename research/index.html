Research

Robotics, intelligence, and real-world systems

Bringing Intelligence into the Physical World

RobAI-Club focuses on robotic systems, embodied intelligence, and reliable autonomous learning. We connect perception, planning, control, learning, and engineering in one closed loop, driven by real-world problems.

What We Explore

  • Robot perception, localization, planning, and control.
  • Embodied intelligence and robot learning.
  • Reliable autonomy for real-world environments.
  • From algorithm prototypes to working robotic systems.

How we work

From Question to System

Understand

Start from real scenarios and define the task, constraints, evaluation criteria, and reproducible experimental setup.

Build

Combine hardware, software, data, and models to turn ideas into prototypes that can be run, measured, and improved.

Validate

Test continuously in simulation and real environments to make systems more robust, interpretable, and useful.
